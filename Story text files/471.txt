This is Natabo. She has six brothers.Her parents give her a small pot. She loves it.One day her brothers break the pot.Natabo cannot find her pot. She cries and cries.Natabo runs away. She climbs a tall tree.Her parents find her. "Please come down, " they ask.Her brothers sing, "Please come down. "Natabo makes fun of their song.Then Natabo's friend comes. "Please come down, " she sings.Natabo's friend sings until she comes down from the tree.The friends walk home together.Everyone celebrates that Natabo is home. Natabo gets a new pot!
