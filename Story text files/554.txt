“Amma, how did you make this roti?” “From a small ball of soft dough.”“And how did you get that dough?” “From this fine white flour.”“And how did you get that flour?” “From this golden brown wheat.”“And how did you get the wheat?” “We grow it in our field.”“How do we grow the wheat?” “ We sow the seeds, water the plants and work hard.” “And then?”“The crop grows and ripens.” “And then?”“We get the wheat and Mona gets her Roti.”“Puffed, hot and delicious.”
