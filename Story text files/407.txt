HOW TO USE THIS BOOK This book contains 14 steps for drawing Supercow, followed by a page to practice tracing Supercow, and overview page of all the steps, and a page for you to draw your final product (after practising). WHAT YOU NEED: You ideally will need: - some scrap paper for practise drawings - pencils, rubber, sharpener for practice drawings -black felt pen/crayon for your final drawing -colouring pencils/felts/crayons or paint for colouring in your final drawing ABOVE ALL, REMEMBER - HAVE FUN!THE ENDMORE BOOKS!
