There once was a boy who could run and jump and climb. He could even make a noise like a seagull. But he couldn’t swim. And oh, how he wanted to.Then one day . . . . . . he noticed a fish that couldn’t swim either.He wanted to help the fish.So he put it in the water . . .. . . and the fish came alive!But the boy also wanted to learn to swim.So the fish showed the boy how to put his mouth in the water and blow.What fun they had!The next day, the boy remembered what the fish had taught him.And so the boy who could run and jump and climb, and even make a noise like a seagull, learned to swim like a fish.
